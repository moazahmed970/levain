Levain opened in 2021 in a converted garage off Al Wasl Road, with one deck oven and a starter Farah carried over from a bakery in Beirut. The idea was narrow on purpose: bake fewer things, bake them the slow way, and stop the day the pans go empty.

Every loaf still goes through the same three hands it did on day one — mixed before dawn, shaped at the bench by the window, and carried out warm to whoever's first in line. No wholesale, no shipping, no second location. Just the counter, the ovens, and the neighbourhood.
